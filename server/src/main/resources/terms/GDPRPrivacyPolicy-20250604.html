What data do we collect?

  • Data that you share with us about your skills, qualification and experience.
  • Personal identification information that you share with us (Name, email address, phone number, etc.)
  • Information about your need for international protection. This allows us to check your eligibility for inclusion on the Talent Catalog.
  • History of any visa applications you have made - including any potential security or health concerns. This allows us to check your potential eligibility for future visas.

How do we collect your data?

We collect the data that you share when you register on the Talent Catalog. You can also update your data at any time by logging in to your account on the Talent Catalog.

You can also provide us with data through interviews with your local Talent Catalog partner. These interviews are called “intakes”.

How will we use your data?

We share your skills, qualifications and experience data so that we can:

  • Show it to employers who might consider hiring you.
  • Show it to organizations that provide services that could help you. For example education and skills training, career planning, settlement services, migration advice, etc

We keep other data private and will only disclose it to others with your permission. In particular, we keep your name and contact details private.

How do we store your data?

We store your data securely on Amazon Web Service's cloud storage.

When will we communicate with you?

We will communicate with you regarding potential employment opportunities and other opportunities offered by organizations wanting to assist refugees and other displaced people.

We may also send you reminders to complete your registration if incomplete, or to complete tasks that are overdue (for example, uploading documents that are needed for your visa application).

What are your data protection rights?

We would like to make sure you are fully aware of all of your data protection rights. Every user is entitled to the following:

The right to access
You can access your personal data any time by logging in to your account on the Talent Catalog.
The right to rectification
You can update your personal data any time by logging in to your account on the Talent Catalog.
The right to erasure
You can delete your personal data any time by logging in to your account on the Talent Catalog.
The right to restrict processing
You are always in control of which opportunities you choose to pursue.
The right to object to processing
You can withdraw from any opportunity at any time.
The right to data portability
You are always in control of your data. Through the Talent Catalog you can view your data, make a copy of your data or approve the sharing of your data with others.

In summary you are in direct control of your data at any time through the Talent Catalog.

Cookies

Cookies are text files placed on your computer when you browse a website.

For further information, see the Wikipedia article on cookies.

How do we use cookies?

The Talent Catalog uses cookies to improve your experience on our website, including:

  • Keeping you signed in
  • Remembering your preferences (for example, preferred language)

This is the common minimal use of cookies that is essential for the basic functioning of any website.

What type of cookies do we use?

There are a number of different types of cookies, however, our website only uses "functional" cookies which are essential for the basic functioning of any website. Functional cookies help us recognize you on our website and remember your previously selected preferences.

How to manage your cookies

You can set your browser not to accept cookies and your browser will also allow you to remove cookies.

However, the Talent Catalog relies on its minimal use cookies for its normal operation so you should accept cookies. That will be the normal default setting of your browser. If you remove cookies you will be logged out of the Talent Catalog (and any other websites you may be logged into). You may also lose any preferences that you have set.

Privacy policies of other websites

The Talent Catalog may contain links to other websites. Our privacy policy applies only to the Talent Catalog and your Talent Catalog partner, so if you click on a link to another website, you should read their privacy policy.

Privacy policies of other Talent Catalog partners

All Talent Catalog partners are required to act according to the same standard privacy policy. If your partner changes, you will be asked to accept a policy with that new partner - but it will be the same policy.

Changes to this privacy policy

You can view this policy at any time by logging in to your Talent Catalog account and selecting Privacy Policy from your account menu. You will also see your Talent Catalog partner there. This privacy policy is a contract between you and your Talent Catalog partner.

Occasionally the privacy policy may change. Or you may change to a different Talent Catalog partner - for example, if you have moved to another country. In either case you will be automatically asked to consent to the latest privacy policy with your current Talent Catalog partner.

How to contact your Talent Catalog partner

If you have any questions about the Talent Catalog privacy policy, the data we hold on you, or if you have questions about your data protection rights, please do not hesitate to contact your Talent Catalog partner. The easiest way to contact them is by logging in to your Talent Catalog account.

How to contact another authority

Should you wish to report a complaint or if you feel that your Talent Catalog partner has not addressed your concern in a satisfactory manner, you should contact your local office of UNHCR, the UN Refugee Agency.
