Talent Beyond Boundaries (TBB) is a nonprofit organization working to open a new safe and legal pathway for individuals fleeing conflict to connect with international employment opportunities in countries where their skills are needed. TBB is implementing a pilot project with those who are displaced and living in Jordan and Lebanon to initially match 30 people with corporations abroad in need of their skills. While only a limited number will be matched with employment initially in order to prove this new pathway is viable, TBB encourages a large number of people who have been displaced from their home country and are living in Lebanon and Jordan to register to show employers the wide range of talent that they can consider in international hiring. After completion of the pilot project, we plan to scale up to connect thousands of displaced people to international employment around the world.

Please continue to update your profile as you gain additional experience, education, or skills.  Talent Beyond Boundaries’ (TBB) mission is to open international visa pathways for refugees and other forcibly displaced people to use their skills and talents as a route towards restored self-reliance and safety. TBB is a nonprofit organization with a cooperation agreement with UNHCR. We link those who are displaced by conflict with jobs in countries around the world where their skills are needed and facilitate the visas for them to work abroad. Anyone is welcome to create a profile on the Talent Catalog; by sharing information about your employment history and skills, you contribute to demonstrating the breadth and depth of talent among displaced people. However, at this time, TBB is only considering applicants currently based in Lebanon and Jordan for positions in Australia, Canada, and the United Kingdom. We will continue to update the Talent Catalog, our website, and our social media with any changes to our program.
