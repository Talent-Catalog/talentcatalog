# Agreement Management

Candidate Portal now supports management of legal agreements between candidates and service providers.

---

### Candidate agreement visibility

Candidates can review the agreements they have made with service providers.

This helps candidates stay aware of their rights and responsibilities when they access services through Talent Catalog.

---

### Supporting service-provider agreements

Agreement management supports automated handling of agreements between candidates and service providers.

This creates a clearer record of which agreements apply to each candidate and helps service-provider workflows remain consistent.

---

### Useful for CASI services

This work supports CASI service flows where a candidate may need to review or accept agreement terms before accessing a service.

It also helps teams manage agreement status more reliably across candidate and service-provider interactions.
