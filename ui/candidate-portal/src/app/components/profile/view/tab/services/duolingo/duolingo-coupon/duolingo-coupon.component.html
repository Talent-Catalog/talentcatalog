What is the Duolingo English Test?

  • You will take the test for free through TBB's partnership with Duolingo
  • An online test that you take at-home with a laptop or desktop computer. You cannot take the test on a tablet or a smartphone.
  • To take the DET you will need to download the Duolingo English Test application on your computer and follow specific rules (explained below).
  • The DET takes 1 hour to complete. It also takes around 30 minutes to set-up.
  • DET questions become easier or harder depending on how you answer them.
  • The DET uses AI technology to monitor you and people who review the video to make sure no rules are broken.
  • You and TBB will receive the results by email after 48 hours.
  1. Block out 2 hours in your calendar. The deadline to take the test is .
  2. Sign up for an account on the DET website using the email you have in your Talent Catalog profile. Check your account dashboard to make sure you have 1 credit available for the test. Reach out to us if you do not.
  3. Download the DET desktop application. You need a computer with either Windows or MacOS and 300MB of free space. Log-in to your account on the application.
  4. Check your inbox for an email from Duolingo with the subject: TBB has invited you to take the Duolingo English Test for free! Click [Accept Offer].
  5. Learn about the DET by watching this video and reading the Readiness Page.
    • Read the test policies, rules and requirements.
      • Note: You may wear a religious head covering that covers your ears while taking the test. Your eyes and mouth must be uncovered and visible.
      • It is important that you follow all the rules when taking the test. This video series also goes over the rules & requirements, and common rules broken. Watch the videos so you know the rules and do not break them.
  6. Take a practice test on the desktop application. Take it at least once, so you become familiar with the test instructions.
  7. Prepare your ID - see an explanation in the attached guide
  8. Take the test! You need a stable internet connection with minimum 2mbps download, 1mbps upload. Avoid using a mobile hotspot, and Duolingo even recommends using an ethernet cable.
  9. You will receive your score after 2 days. You can understand how your score compares to other tests like the IELTS by checking out this page.

**IMPORTANT: If you face technical problems, email us and wait for a reply. DO NOT purchase a test. We will send you another free coupon.**

Good luck!
